About Us

Vilats Global is an education-focused organization passionate about helping people learn, grow, and succeed. We work across a wide range of areas—from language learning and academic courses to vocational training and skill development programs.

Whether online or offline, we create engaging content and digital tools to support every kind of learner. We also assist educators through training, research, and consultancy to enhance the quality of education. Collaboration is key—we work with institutions in India and internationally to share knowledge and build better learning experiences.

Through workshops, seminars, and events, we bring communities together to exchange ideas and inspire growth. Above all, we believe in making education inclusive, practical, and innovative for everyone.

Directors

K. Preetham

K. Preetham completed a Master’s in English Literature in 2008 with a university rank from Bangalore University. He qualified KSET in 2015 and is a Cambridge University-certified oral examiner for the Business English Certification (BEC) exam. He has presented papers at 37 national and international conferences, with 7 research papers published in UGC CARE Journals and 1 in a peer-reviewed journal. Additionally, 5 of his articles have been published as chapters in books and magazines.

He has delivered talks as a resource person at a national-level FDP and an international conference. His academic achievements include completing 6 SWAYAM FDP courses on research and English skills, and 44 international online courses in English from reputed global universities. He has participated in over 200 webinars.

With over 12 years of teaching and training experience across undergraduate, graduate, and postgraduate levels (B.Sc, B.E, MBA), he has trained students for verbal aptitude in competitive exams like CAT and also coached IT and non-IT professionals. He has worked as a Subject Matter Expert for HirePro, developing verbal aptitude content, and as a Senior Content Developer at Halis, where he created content for international certification courses like ITIL and PRINCE2. He is also a certified instructional designer.

He actively shares knowledge through his YouTube and Telegram channels related to English Language and Literature. His research articles can be found on ResearchGate and Google Scholar.

Dr. Harshitha Y.S

Dr. Harshitha Y.S earned her Ph.D. in 2020 from the University of Mysore with a thesis titled “Youth Buying Behavior of Fast Food from Select Chains of Outlets in Karnataka.” She qualified KSET in 2016, completed her PGDHRM in 2014 from KSOU, and earned her MBA in Marketing from B.N. Bahadur Institute of Management and Science, Mysore, in the same year.

She has presented papers at 7 national and international conferences and published 2 research papers in UGC CARE Journals, 1 in Scopus, 4 in peer-reviewed journals, and 1 as a book chapter with ISBN. She has attended over 16 national and international webinars, seminars, and workshops.

With around 7 years of teaching and training experience, she has taught at undergraduate and postgraduate levels, particularly in BBA and M.Com programs.
